Main Arabic Hadis r Onubad Dilam

- Onubader moddhe sonod ta bad disi, so sonod check korar dorkar nai.Main hadith ta check korba only.
- Now onubad same rekhe arabic er sathe match kore onubad/banan/missing etc kono problem thakle fix kore dao
- Strictly main onubad same rakhte hobe, only corrected onubad ta dao, extra kono text diba na
- Kothao (আরবি) lekha pele just keep it no change needed. dont give arabics by yourself.
- Also oprocholito bangla banan gulao fix korba like ওয়াহী > ওহী, ফিরিশতা > ফেরেশতা etc.
- STRICTLY (সাল্লাল্লাহু 'আলাইহি ওয়া সাল্লাম), (রাঃ) ei types word gula change korba na
- Also songjukto vul banan gulo fix korba ex: কাফ্ফারা > কাফ্‌ফারা, আম্র > আম্‌র etc.
- Sahabider namer banan vul thakle fix korba
- Strictly extra kono text diba na
